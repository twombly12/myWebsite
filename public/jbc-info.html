Project Overview

What do I get?

At the end of this project you will receive;

  • Logo - A Logo recolored in up to 4 variations. These are;
    • The primary coloring of your Logo
    • The inverted coloring of your Logo
    • An All-Black version of your Logo
    • An All-White version of your Logo
  • Logo Files - 4 sets of File Types for each color variation
    • 1 set of PNG’s with transparent backgrounds in the RGB color scheme (for digital materials)
    • 1 set of JPG’s with colored backgrounds in the RGB color scheme (for digital materials)
    • 1 set of PNGS’s with transparent backgrounds in the CMYK scheme (for printed materials)
    • 1 set of EPS’s (for professional usage)
  • File Details - Details on how to use the different file types for your Logo
  • Color Codes - The Color Codes for your Logos (Provided as HEX codes)
  • Fonts - The Fonts used in your Logo
  • Business Card Design - A PDF for your Business Card Design
  • Business Card Printing & Shipping - 500 copies of your Business Cards printed & Shipped to you
  • Letterhead Design - A PDF for your Letterhead Design
  • Letterhead Printing & Shipping - 250 copies of your Letterhead printed & Shipped to you
  • Marketing Video - A copy of the JBC Marketing Video rebranded with your Logo, your Colors, and your Contact Info
  • Website - Your own JBC Website rebranded with your Logo, your Colors, and your Contact Info
    • Design & Development of Desktop, Tablet & Mobile Website
    • JBC Approved content with all of the info your customers will need
    • Sales Funnel created by Digital Marketing Team
    • Optimized by SEO expert for Google Searches
    • Google Analytics Configuration & Implementation - This allows you to see the traffic to your website
    • Google Search Console Configuration & Implementation - This allows you to see the types of Google Searches that you appear for
    • SSL Security License setup & installation for your Site
    • 1 year of Web Hosting included
    • 1 year of Domain Name included

What does all of this cost?

Nothing! Everything is included in your Operator Package

Logo Info

What will I receive with my Logo?

Please refer to the Project Overview at the top of this page

How do I get started on my Logo?

You can begin the Logo Process by submitting the Intake Form that was provided in your introductory email

Do I need to have my Business Name registered before I fill out the Logo Intake Form?

Yes, please ensure that your Business Name is registered before you begin the process for your Logo. Changing your Business Name partway through the Logo process will count as a round of revisions.

Are the questions on the Intake Form important?

Extremely! The more information that you provide in the Logo Intake Form, the better we can understand who you are, what your style is, and what type of logo might suit you. This makes it more likely that you will receive something that resonates with you personally, and is something that you wil be excited about. We understand that you may not have the answers to all of the questions, but please try your best.

In particular, give extra attention to these questions;

  • What is the Origin of your Business Name?
  • Who are your competitors, and what do you think of their Logos?
  • What Colors would you like to see in your Logo?
  • Please provide links to a few Logos that you Love.

When do I have to submit my Logo Intake Form by?

You only have to submit your answers to the form once you are ready to begin. However, please let us know that you have received the Introduction Email with the Logo Form Intake link, so we know that it didn’t get lost in your Spam / Junk folder.

What happens after I submit my Intake Form?

Once you have submitted your Intake Form, we will reach out to you within 1 Business Day to confirm receipt. At this point, we will have everything we need to begin your Logo, and we will get to work! We will have the initial draft of Logo Concepts ready for you within 7 days

How many Logo Concepts will I receive?

The initial drafts for your Logo will contain 3-4 Logo Concepts for you to review

Can I make changes to the Logo Concepts?

Once you have received the initial drafts for your Logo Concepts, you can request revisions and adjustments to the concepts

How many revisions can I request?

You can request adjustments to 2-3 concepts per round of revision, and we can do up to 3 rounds of revisions

How do I send the request for my revisions?

All revision requests must be sent by email to your designer

Providing reference material is incredibly beneficial for both parties! This is epecially true if you have specific colors or design styles that you would like to emulate

Is there a phone number that I can call?

Unfortunately, phone calls cannot be accepted to discuss the Logo Concepts or revisions. Due to the visual nature of the work, everything must be provided via email. Text messages, Social Media Message, etc... cannot be accepted either.

Business Card & Letterhead Info

What will I receive with my Business Cards & Letterhead?

Please refer to the Project Overview at the top of this page

Can I have Business Cards printed for multiple Operators?

Definitely! We will just need you to provide the contact information for each operator in the Logo Form. If you have more than 2 Operators, please email the contact information for the additional operators after you have submitted your Logo Intake Form. The 500 printed Business Cards will be divided amongst the Operators.

When do you create my Business Cards & Letterhead?

Once your Logo is completed, we will re-confirm your Contact Info with you for your Business Cards and Letterhead.

After your Contact Info is confirmed, we will create your Business Cards & Letterhead

Will I get to review my Business Cards & Letterhead before they are printed?

Yes, we will send your Business Cards & Letterhead to you for approval before they are sent to the printing house

When will I receive my Business Cards & Letterhead?

Once you have approved your Business Cards & Letterhead, we will send them to the printing house. Printing and shipping for the Business Cards & Letterhead can take 3-5 weeks

Can the digital files for the Business Card & Letterhead be provided in a format other than PDF?

We cannot provide the Business Cards & Letterhead files in a format other than PDF

What if I want to use my Business Cards & Letterhead Files in Word?

Here are a few options for using your Business Cards & Letterhead Files in Word are;

  • Export the PDF to a Word Document (Tutorial)
  • Convert your PDF to an Image, and add it as background to your Word Document (Tutorial)
  • Preprint your Letterhead, and load it into your paper tray (Tutorial)
  • Open the PDF as a Word Document (Tutorial)

Some of these solutions may not work depending on your Operating System, or the version of Word that you are using (Word barely plays nicely with itself at the best of times). The best options are to convert the PDF to an image and use it as a background, or to preprint your letterhead.
